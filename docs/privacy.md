# PGSync Pro — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: 22 July 2026*

We keep this short because the product is built to keep your data in your own
hands.

## The product collects no telemetry

PGSync Pro runs entirely in **your** infrastructure. It does **not** phone home
and sends us **no** usage data, document contents, queries, or metrics. With the
default local / FastEmbed providers, text is embedded on-device and never leaves
your environment.

If you opt into a hosted embedding provider (OpenAI, Cohere, Voyage), only the
fields you configure as the embedding source are sent to **that** provider, under
their terms — not to us.

## What we do collect (buying & support)

To sell and support the Software we process:

- **Billing data** — handled by **Polar**, our payment provider acting as
  Merchant of Record; we receive your name, organization, email, country, and
  subscription status. We do not store card details.
- **Account / delivery** — the email and organization used to issue your
  private-index token.
- **Support correspondence** — anything you send us when you contact support.

We use this only to provide, bill for, and support the Software, and we do not
sell it. Sub-processors: **Polar** (payments / checkout) and **Gemfury** (private
package index).
